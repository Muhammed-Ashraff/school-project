Welcome to Forcados High School

The Forcados High School aims at investing in the Nigerian children for the good of the society. We have both primary and secondary sections. Our school has a reputation of high moral and academic standards. We have been able to achieve these through our mission: ‘partnership with the parents to give an all-round education to the students, based on the dignity of the human person, integrity, leadership qualities and academic excellence’ and our vision: ‘Achieve unity in diversity’.

The Forcados High School


VISION

To see every Forcados student equipped with an integral education which enables her to play her unique role in the home, work place and the larger society.

MISSION

Partnership with parents to give an all-round education to each student, based on societal norms and principles, with emphasis on the dignity of the human person, integrity, leadership qualities and academic excellence.

CORE VALUES

Freedom with Responsibility Dignity of Work Responsible use of Resources Spirit of Service Parents involvement as Primary Educators
